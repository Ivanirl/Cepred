Who we are

The Centre for Population Research and Development (CEPRED) is a centre established with a mission to provide standard health care according to international protocols and guidelines to Nigerians. Formed in January 2011, the centre is poised to grow rapidly and she combines proximity to people who require services; collaborative efforts and expertise to deliver world class services.
